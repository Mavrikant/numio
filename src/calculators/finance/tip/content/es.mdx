# Calculadora de Propina — Dividir la Cuenta y Calcular la Propina

This free tip calculator helps you determine the tip amount, total bill, and per-person share for your group.

## What is a Tip / Gratuity?

A tip is a voluntary payment to a service worker as recognition of good service. Customs vary widely by country and culture. This calculator works for any scenario where you need to calculate and split a service-related payment.

**Formula:**

> Tip Amount = Bill × (Tip% ÷ 100)
> Total = Bill + Tip
> Per Person = Total ÷ Number of People

### The Round-Up Method

When paying cash, rounding each person's share up to the nearest whole currency unit simplifies the payment. Our calculator shows both exact and rounded amounts.

## How to Use

1. Enter the bill amount (subtotal before tip).
2. Set the tip percentage (15–20% is standard in the US; adjust for local customs).
3. Enter the number of people sharing the bill.
4. Toggle round-up for easy cash payment.
5. Click Calculate.

## FAQ

### How much tip is standard?

In the US: 15% (acceptable), 18% (good service), 20–25% (excellent). In many European countries, 5–10% is common but not mandatory. In Japan, tipping is generally not practiced.

### Should I tip on pre-tax or post-tax?

Etiquette experts recommend tipping on the pre-tax subtotal, though many people tip on the total including tax.

### What if a service charge is already included?

Check your bill carefully. If a service charge or gratuity is already added, you are not obligated to tip again.

### How do I split unevenly?

This calculator splits evenly. For uneven splits, ask your server for separate checks or use the per-person tip as a guide.

### Is tip income taxable?

In the US, yes — all tips are fully taxable income that must be reported to the IRS.
